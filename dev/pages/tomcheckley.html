Tom Checkley Web Development

This site is my most recent. I have made many, many versions and never been quite happy with them. I have finally settled on a design (for now). I've used a lot of techniques that I have used in other sites previously, and also learned some new ones. For example the parallax nav bar at the top of the page.

I made fallbacks for devices not showing Javascript, and used some new technologies such as background blend mode with javascrip fallbacks for browsers not currently supporting.

The site utilises the 12 column grid system I developed with bones. It also uses flexbox, which looking at can I use seems to be virtually fully supported with prefixes in older browsers.
