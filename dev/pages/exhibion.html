Exhibion

Exhibion (and yes, the spelling is correct!) is a local artist who heard about me through a mutual friend. He asked me to create him a site on fairly short notice for an upcoming exhibition of his work.

It was fun to work on and test myself under pressure. It also gave me a good insight into working with a 'proper' client, especially as the design was changed a number of times before the due date.

I used a few new techniques on this site as well as my Bones framework. The gallery was more complicated than I think it necessarily needed to be, however it was interesting to use jQuery .data to populate the information on the lightbox view.
